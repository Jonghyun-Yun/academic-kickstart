# Jonghyun Yun is an Assistant Professor in the [[https://www.uta.edu/math/][Department of Mathematics]] at UT Arlington.
He completed his PhD in the Department of Statistics at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign under the guidance of [[https://publish.illinois.edu/yuguo/][Dr. Yuguo Chen]]. Prior to joining UTA, he was an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematical Sciences and a core faculty in the Border Biomedical Research Center at the University of Texas at El Paso, from 2015 to 2016. He was a postdoctoral researcher at UT Southwestern Medical Center under the guidance of Drs. [[https://qbrc.swmed.edu/labs/xiaolab/][Guanghua Xiao]] and [[https://qbrc.swmed.edu/labs/xielab/][Yang Xie]] from 2012 to 2015.
